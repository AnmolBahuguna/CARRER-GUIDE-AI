Career Growth Roadmap

Chart your path to success in tech - from entry-level to leadership

1

Junior/Entry Level

0-2 Years Experience

Start your journey with foundational skills and hands-on experience. Focus on learning core technologies, best practices, and building your first projects.

JavaScript React.js Node.js Git HTML/CSS

Key Responsibilities:

  • Write clean, maintainable code following best practices
  • Participate in code reviews and learn from feedback
  • Fix bugs and implement small features
  • Collaborate with team members on projects
  • Learn and adapt to new technologies quickly
Salary Range: ₹5-12 LPA
2

Mid-Level Developer

2-4 Years Experience

Take on more complex challenges, lead small features, and mentor junior developers. Build expertise in specific domains and contribute to architectural decisions.

TypeScript AWS Docker System Design Testing

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement complex features independently
  • Mentor junior developers and conduct code reviews
  • Participate in technical decision-making
  • Optimize application performance and scalability
  • Contribute to architecture and design discussions
Salary Range: ₹12-25 LPA
3

Senior Developer

4-6 Years Experience

Drive technical excellence, lead major initiatives, and make critical architectural decisions. Become a go-to expert in your domain.

Microservices Kubernetes CI/CD Performance Leadership

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead major technical initiatives and projects
  • Design scalable and maintainable system architectures
  • Make critical technical decisions and trade-offs
  • Mentor mid-level and junior developers
  • Drive best practices and technical standards
Salary Range: ₹25-40 LPA
4

Lead/Principal Engineer

6-8 Years Experience

Shape the technical direction of products, lead multiple teams, and drive innovation. Focus on strategic technical decisions and organizational impact.

Architecture Strategy Innovation Multi-team Research

Key Responsibilities:

  • Define technical vision and strategy for products
  • Lead multiple engineering teams and initiatives
  • Drive innovation and research new technologies
  • Influence technical decisions across the organization
  • Represent engineering in executive discussions
Salary Range: ₹40-70 LPA
5

Manager/Architect

8+ Years Experience

Lead at the highest level, manage engineering organizations, and drive business impact through technology. Balance technical excellence with business strategy.

Leadership Strategy Business Organization Vision

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead large engineering organizations and teams
  • Drive business strategy through technology
  • Build and scale engineering culture
  • Make critical business and technical decisions
  • Mentor future leaders and architects
Salary Range: ₹70+ LPA
